Project Yellow Light is a National Competition meant to encourage teenagers from distracted driving. Every year PYL encourages students from around the country to submit their own advertising executions - in the form of billboards, commercials, and radio spots - into the competiton. In 2019, my billboard design was selected out of 1500 different submissions and displayed in Time Square as well as on highways across the nation, courtesy of Clear Channel Outdoor.

My execution was based in the finding that teenagers often do not like to text and drive but do so to seem 'cool' in front of their peers. This is what inspired by copy and my overall design execution was inspired by teenagers' reliance on social media to find satisfaction and reassurance from their peers.
