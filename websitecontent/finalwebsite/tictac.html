Tic Tac is a brand of mouth mints. My original concept was for one print ad but that idea quickly transformed into a larger camapaign that included a digital execution as well. The entire Campaign was built off of the idea that Tic Tac is here to keep your mouth fresh, and it can keep your love life fresh too!

In hopes of taking this concept one step further - I developed the Fresh Date Generator App sponsored by Tic Tac. This digital extension makes that possible with the ‘Fresh Date Generator’ in which, after scanning the QR code and answering just three questions, Tic Tac will recommend the perfect one-of-a-kind date for you and your significant other.
