This digital execution aims to overcome Crosley’s challenge of marketing vinyl records to an audience that grew up with music on demand. Having the world’s musical library at the tip of their fingertips has also created a deep admiration for music as a whole among Generation Z.

Which is why I created a brand collaboration between Crosley and Spotify. Through this collaboration, Spotify users will be able to select their favorite Spotify playlist and turn it into their very own custom Crosly vinyl. Turning their favorite playlist into one that will last a lifetime. This execution combines the ease of digitla streaming services such as Spotify with the pricelss value of a vinyl record, potentially introducing a new generation to the value of this authentic medium.
